Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • No paid advertising observed across Google Ads, Local Service Ads, or Meta — all three channels unoccupied while competitors actively spend
  • Organic presence exists with geo-targeting pages for Detroit and Southfield, but no system to capture active buyers who are searching right now
  • Lead sources not identified on the call — the firm has no documented, predictable system for attracting new consumer protection clients
Without a reliable way to bring in clients, the firm's revenue — and Adam's personal freedom — will always depend on what happens to show up.

Intake

  • Adam personally handles all intake — there is no process that functions in his absence, no delegation, and no after-hours coverage
  • Current intake service costs $1,500/month but operates without AI-backed automation or consistent follow-up systems
  • No structured follow-up sequence for prospects who do not sign immediately — leads that do not convert on first contact are likely lost
Every inquiry that does not convert is a case Adam cannot recover with personal time — the revenue loss is also a freedom loss.

Self-Managing Team

  • Adam is the firm — sole practitioner with no staff, no processes others could execute, and no delegation of any kind in place
  • Fear of hiring has previously prevented growth: Adam has hesitated to bring on staff and to partner with SMB Team due to the perceived management burden
  • With all operations manual and no documented systems, the firm cannot be delegated to or sold at meaningful value in its current state
A firm that can only run when Adam is running it is not an asset — it is a job, and a stressful one.

Profit

  • Revenue has grown consistently — from approximately $500k to over $600k last year, targeting $900k this year — real momentum is in place
  • All financial processes are manual with no tracking software, no margin visibility, and no clarity on what Adam personally keeps after expenses
  • Without structured financial systems, revenue growth may not translate into proportional increases in personal income or firm value
Knowing exactly what the firm earns and what Adam keeps is the foundation for making freedom a math problem, not a guess.
