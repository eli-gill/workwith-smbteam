Pillar Health Scorecard

Lead Generation

  • All leads come from referrals, newspaper ads, and grocery cart ads — no digital system exists
  • Google Business Profile is present but under-optimized; firm does not appear in local search for estate planning or probate keywords
  • No Google LSA, no paid search, and no Meta advertising has ever been activated — an untapped market sits unclaimed
Without a predictable digital lead system, hitting 10–20 new cases per month in the next 6 months is not possible — and the campaign fund depends on it.

Intake

  • Amanda personally handles every intake conversation while managing all casework — no dedicated intake process or follow-up system
  • No after-hours coverage; calls outside business hours go unanswered and are frequently lost to competitors
  • No call tracking or CRM to measure which leads converted and which were lost — there is no visibility into the gap
Every unanswered call and every slow follow-up is revenue that will never make it to the campaign fund or the financial cushion for January.

Self-Managing Team

  • Jennifer, the paralegal of 16 years, brings deep institutional knowledge — a genuine asset most solo firms do not have
  • With only two people in the firm, Amanda's personal availability is the ceiling on case volume and coverage
  • No formal delegation structure; if Amanda is in court, at a campaign event, or unavailable, intake and operations stall
The 6-month campaign trail will compete directly with firm operations — the team needs to be structured so Amanda can be in two places at once.

Profit

  • Revenue is stable at ~$350,000/year, but two known margin leaks exist: probate is billed at $250/hr and paralegal time is not billed at all
  • No formal profit plan, financial KPIs, or visibility into per-case profitability by practice area
  • With 6 months to maximize take-home, pricing discipline and paralegal billing are immediate levers worth thousands per month
Recovering margin on the cases already being handled funds the campaign and the January transition without requiring a single new client.
