Intake

  • 52+ leads have been sitting in MyCase since January with only a single voicemail left and no further follow-up — an estimated $50,000+ in potential revenue sitting idle

Every dollar that leaks through intake is a dollar Jason cannot reinvest in the attorney hire that would free him from the court-appearance bottleneck.

Self-Managing Team

  • Jason is the firm — every court appearance, every high-value case, every key decision runs through him personally
  • The one assistant is not performing to standard: scripts ignored, critical data fields left blank, follow-up queues neglected despite coaching
  • No KPIs, no scorecards, and no accountability structure exist — there is nothing to hold the team to a measurable standard
Until Jason has a team that runs without constant correction, he cannot step into the "Business Owner" role he explicitly said is the goal.

Profit

  • No formal bookkeeping or financial projections — Jason manages finances reactively, without data on margins, cost per acquisition, or practice area profitability
  • Revenue is estimated at approximately $400,000 annually — but without financial tracking, there is no visibility into how much is actually being kept
  • Hiring decisions (attorney, part-time staffer) are being made without financial modeling — the wrong hire at the wrong time could erode margins significantly
A firm you can eventually step back from — and sell — requires predictable, documented profit. Right now that foundation does not exist.
