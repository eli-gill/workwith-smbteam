Here Is Where Adler Law Firm Stands Today

Steven Adler has spent 17 years building a respected estate planning and probate practice in Jericho, New York — generating $1.4 million per year with a team of eight and over $10,000 per month invested in Scorpion digital marketing. The firm is producing more than 100 internet leads per month, yet a consultation-to-client conversion rate that has fallen from 90% to 60% means dozens of paid leads are walking away every month without becoming clients.

Stage 6 is the stage where Steven works 2.5 days a week, Marissa runs the day-to-day, and the firm generates consistent income without requiring his constant presence — and that is exactly where this plan takes him.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • Scorpion marketing generates 100+ internet leads per month — but intake converts only 60%, down from 90%, costing an estimated 1–5 cases per month at $6,000 each
  • Steven personally handles banking and administrative tasks that have not been delegated to anyone on the team
  • A team of 8 attorneys and paralegals operates without KPIs, scorecards, or a structured accountability system
  • Firm finances are tracked manually in Excel with a broken Clio–QuickBooks sync — no real-time financial visibility exists

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between working four days a week and working two and a half is not as wide as it feels — but closing it requires systems, not just effort.
