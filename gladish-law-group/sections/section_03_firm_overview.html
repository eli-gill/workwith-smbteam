Here Is Where Gladish Law Group Stands Today

Gladish Law Group is a three-attorney personal injury and civil trial firm headquartered in Highland, Indiana — with a second office in Crown Point — that has built an exceptional courtroom reputation over three decades, securing more than $82 million in verdicts and settlements for clients across Northwest Indiana and Illinois. Founder David Gladish holds the highest credentials in plaintiff personal injury law: Board Certified Civil Trial Specialist, Indiana SuperLawyers member every year since 2010, Best Lawyers in America, and a lifetime Million Dollar Advocate. The single most important finding from this audit: the firm's reputation and results are outstanding — but they are not being amplified by any paid digital marketing, while more aggressive competitors are actively investing in every channel where injured clients are searching right now.

Stage 6 is where Gladish Law Group gets to stop winning cases through David's personal reputation and starts winning them through a system that runs on its own — so David can focus on the trial work he does best, or step back and let the firm carry on without him.

Where You Are Today

Stage 4: Small Business Manager
  • Firm growth is powered by David Gladish's personal reputation and referrals — not a scalable, owned marketing system
  • No Google Ads, Local Service Ads, or Meta Ads campaigns observed — competitors are occupying those positions daily
  • 266 Google reviews at 4.8 stars — strong quality signal, but far outpaced by regional competitors with thousands of reviews
  • No dedicated intake team or confirmed intake process — likely attorney-handled, creating a ceiling on how many leads can be managed

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between where Gladish Law Group is and where it deserves to be is not a talent gap or a reputation gap — it is a marketing and systems gap, and that is entirely fixable.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
Gladish Law Group
Gladish Law Group is operating at Stage 4 — a well-established Small Business Manager with exceptional trial credentials and a hard-earned reputation in Northwest Indiana, but without a systematic digital marketing engine to match that reputation in the market. The single most urgent limiting factor is lead generation visibility: while Hilbrich Law Firm operates on the same street in Highland, Lerner & Rowe invests aggressively across every digital channel, and Stracci Law Group brands itself as the region's #1 firm, Gladish Law Group does not appear to be running a single paid advertising campaign. That means the firm is winning only the cases that find it on their own — not the cases that are actively searching for it every day. Until a predictable marketing system is built alongside a defined intake process, growth will remain dependent on David Gladish's personal bandwidth — which is both the ceiling and the opportunity.
