Lead Generation

McLellan Law Group, LLP has built meaningful credibility in the Saratoga market — two nationally recognized Rising Star attorneys, an active blog, and a multi-platform social presence — but the firm's lead flow is almost entirely dependent on Law Broker, a single shared intake channel converting at only 8–10%, with no Google Ads presence, no Local Service Ads, and limited organic visibility against well-resourced San Jose competitors that are actively investing in all of these channels.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • The homepage CTA ("Request a Free Consultation") links to an external Law Broker intake form at lawbrokr.com — every visitor who clicks to inquire is taken off mclellanlawgroup.com, adding friction and preventing the firm from owning its own prospect data
  • PageSpeed scores: scores were not obtained during research — run pagespeed.web.dev before the sales call and insert mobile and desktop scores here
  • No video content is embedded on the homepage — the firm produces AI avatar video content for TikTok, Meta, and YouTube, but none of it appears on the website where it could build trust, increase dwell time, and improve organic rankings
  • No geo-targeting landing pages for surrounding cities — San Jose, Campbell, Los Gatos, Los Altos, Palo Alto, and Cupertino are all within the firm's service area and high-net-worth concentrations, but have no dedicated pages capturing location-specific searches
Blind Spot: The firm's CTA links directly to Law Broker (lawbrokr.com) — when visitors click it, they leave the mclellanlawgroup.com domain entirely. Prospects who abandon the Law Broker form mid-way cannot be retargeted, and no data is captured for Google Ads or Meta retargeting audiences. Every ad dollar spent driving traffic to the website effectively loses its ability to follow up with unconverted visitors.
The Positive
  • Attorney bios are present for all three attorneys — a key trust signal that many small firms fail to include, and one that helps prospects make the decision to reach out
  • An active blog with posts through January 2026 and multiple practice area categories demonstrates content commitment and creates SEO surface area for organic discovery
The Gap
  • Sending visitors off-site to Law Broker means the firm cannot build retargeting audiences — paid Google or Meta campaigns lose a critical remarketing signal every time a prospect leaves the domain
  • No video on the website means the firm's content investment does not benefit organic rankings — video increases average dwell time and engagement, both signals that Google uses to rank pages
  • No city-specific landing pages limits organic visibility for high-intent, location-specific searches in premium adjacent markets where clients with high-value disputes are actively searching
Every improvement to this website turns the firm's existing credibility into clients — without adding a single hour of partner time or a dollar of additional spend.

Local SEO

The Problem
  • The firm's Google Business Profile review count could not be confirmed via search — the firm appears to have a minimal Google review presence, which is the primary ranking signal for local 3-pack placement; competitors Costanzo Law Firm (69 reviews, 4.3★), Structure Law Group (27 reviews, 5.0★), and Berliner Cohen (7 reviews, 5.0★) all have meaningful review authority while McLellan has virtually none
  • The firm does not appear in the local 3-pack for competitive San Jose keywords including "business litigation attorney San Jose," "employment attorney San Jose CA," and "real estate litigation attorney San Jose" — San Jose firms with stronger GBP authority dominate these results
  • NAP inconsistencies across directories: Avvo lists the firm under both Saratoga CA 95070 and Campbell CA 95008, and the Facebook page is tagged as Campbell CA — conflicting location data signals suppress Google Business Profile authority and local ranking ability
Blind Spot: The AEO audit referenced on the discovery call confirmed McLellan already ranks for specific queries including "civil litigation services for mid-sized real estate companies" — and there is currently no clear winner for "best asset protection attorney for high net worth individuals." This is a high-value niche keyword that could be claimed with targeted content and a strong Google review base. The firm has an AEO advantage it is not yet converting into leads.
The Positive
  • The firm is one of the only civil litigation boutiques physically based in Saratoga — for Saratoga-specific queries, the firm's address gives it a proximity advantage that San Jose firms cannot replicate
The Gap
  • Without a strong Google review count, the firm is invisible in the local pack for San Jose searches that drive the majority of high-value business litigation volume in the Silicon Valley market
  • NAP inconsistencies across Avvo and Facebook send conflicting location signals to Google, suppressing GBP authority and reducing the firm's ability to rank in any local pack
Costanzo Law Firm, APC — 69 Google reviews at 4.3 stars give them substantial local pack authority across employment law queries in San Jose, where McLellan has minimal Google review visibility and no confirmed 3-pack presence.
A firm that owns the local 3-pack for business litigation and employment keywords in the San Jose metro generates clients without the partners personally chasing each one.

Google Ads

Ad Presence
  • McLellan Law Group does not appear to be running Google search ads for business litigation, employment, or real estate litigation terms in the San Jose and Saratoga market — the firm may not have configured these campaigns or may be getting outbid by larger San Jose competitors actively investing in paid search
  • The discovery call confirmed the firm has been hesitant to invest in paid search due to cost concerns — this hesitancy has left the firm without an exclusive, controllable lead source beyond a shared marketplace that converts at 8–10%
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Structure Law Group, LLP — active across business litigation and corporate law search terms in San Jose and Silicon Valley; strong presence in both organic and paid results for Silicon Valley business dispute keywords
  • Costanzo Law Firm, APC — highly visible in San Jose employment attorney searches with prominent placement across employment law and discrimination keywords
  • Berliner Cohen, LLP — full-service business litigation firm with active search presence for commercial dispute and employment law keywords across the San Jose metro
The Opportunity
  • High-intent practice area searches: "business litigation attorney San Jose CA," "employment defense attorney Bay Area," "civil litigation lawyer Saratoga CA," "real estate dispute attorney San Jose," "contract dispute attorney Silicon Valley"
  • High-net-worth niche: "asset protection attorney high net worth California," "best asset protection attorney for executives" — the AEO audit confirmed no clear winner for these terms, making this a low-competition paid search opportunity
  • Adjacent city targeting: Campbell, Los Gatos, Los Altos, Cupertino, Palo Alto — these communities have high concentrations of business owners and executives with the kinds of disputes this firm handles, and lower ad competition than central San Jose
  • Competitor name targeting: bidding on searches for Costanzo Law Firm, Structure Law Group, and Silicon Valley Law Group captures prospects who are already researching local alternatives and are actively in the decision phase
Blind Spot: Law Broker is a shared marketplace — McLellan competes for prospects against other attorneys on the platform, and those leads are not exclusive. Google Ads delivers leads exclusively to McLellan. Every dollar spent on Google Ads builds the firm's own pipeline rather than renting someone else's.

Local Service Ads

LSA Presence
  • McLellan Law Group does not appear to be Google Screened or running Local Service Ads — the firm is absent from the highest-trust advertising position on Google, which appears above all paid search ads, above the map pack, and above all organic results
  • LSA eligibility for civil litigation and employment law requires a verified Google review foundation — building that foundation now is the prerequisite for activating this channel
Competitor LSA Landscape
  • Costanzo Law Firm, APC — 69 Google reviews at 4.3 stars; strong review authority positions them for prominent LSA placement in San Jose employment attorney searches
  • Structure Law Group, LLP — 27 Google reviews at 5.0 stars; their perfect rating creates a strong LSA candidacy for business litigation and employment terms in Silicon Valley
The Opportunity
  • Building 20–30 verified Google reviews in the next 60 days creates LSA eligibility and meaningfully improves local pack visibility for Saratoga-specific searches simultaneously
  • LSAs appear above everything else on Google — above paid ads, above the map pack, above all organic results — and you only pay when a real prospect calls you directly.
  • For civil litigation and employment matters billed at $400–450/hour, a single case generated from LSA can produce $5,000–$15,000+ in billings — making the cost-per-lead economics exceptionally strong for this practice area

Facebook & Instagram Ads

Ad Presence
  • McLellan Law Group does not appear to be running active paid Meta ads — the firm produces organic video content (AI avatar videos) on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, but the discovery call confirmed these organic posts are generating no client conversions
  • Organic social video is not functioning as a lead source; paid distribution is the mechanism that turns content into booked consultations — without it, the firm's content investment produces impressions, not clients
Competitor Ad Landscape
  • Structure Law Group, LLP — active Facebook presence with consistent content; using paid distribution to amplify reach across the San Jose metro business owner audience
  • Berliner Cohen, LLP — active Facebook presence investing in multi-channel visibility across the Silicon Valley legal market
The Opportunity
  • Retargeting — website visitors who did not convert are leaving with no follow-up
  • Video ads featuring the attorney — builds trust before a prospect ever calls
  • Educational content ads — capture leads earlier in the decision process
  • The firm already produces AI avatar video content — this content can be repurposed immediately as paid Meta video ads targeting high-net-worth business owners and executives in Silicon Valley who are in the research phase of selecting a civil litigation attorney; turning existing content into a paid channel requires no new production
