Here Is Where McLellan Law Group, LLP Stands Today

Stage 6 is where the firm generates revenue, serves clients, and grows — without requiring the partners to be present for every decision. That is the system you described wanting to build, and it is exactly where this process is designed to take you.

Overall Growth Stage Assessment
McLellan Law Group, LLP
McLellan Law Group, LLP is operating at Stage 4 — a Small Business Manager with real revenue, a growing team, and one scalable lead system already in place, but without the multi-channel engine or operational infrastructure needed to reach $600,000. The single most urgent limitation is lead generation: the firm has no Google Ads presence, no Local Service Ad presence, minimal Google review authority, and a primary intake funnel converting at only 8–10% in a market where established San Jose competitors are actively investing in paid search and local SEO. Until the firm builds a predictable, multi-channel lead engine and fixes the intake system that is losing 90% of prospects it is already generating, the revenue goal — and the freedom Claire described wanting — will remain just out of reach.
