Here Is Where PS Law Group Stands Today

PS Law Group is a specialized B2B law firm founded in 2021 by Paula Savchenko in Wilton Manors, Florida, serving regulated industries — cannabis, hemp, kratom, dietary supplements, and tobacco — across eleven states. With revenue growing from $800K to approximately $1 million annually, the firm is at a critical inflection point: Paula herself has become both the firm's greatest asset and its most urgent bottleneck, personally handling every client call, billing decision, case review, and operational task while working 60+ hours per week — with no system in place to convert her expertise into predictable, scalable growth.

Stage 6 is the stage where the firm runs without you — where you can build the AI company you've been imagining, expand into autonomous vehicles, and finally stop being the ceiling on your own growth.

Where You Are Today

Stage 3: Solo Practitioner
  • Every new client comes through word-of-mouth — no paid advertising, no SEO strategy, no predictable inbound system
  • Paula personally handles all client calls, billing, case review, and admin — working 60+ hours per week with no delegation structure
  • No per-case profitability data means hiring and investment decisions are made on gut feel, not financial clarity
  • Monthly revenue ranges from $50K to $100K+ — the inconsistency makes planning a 10-attorney firm nearly impossible

Where You Need To Be

Stage 6: Law Firm Owner
  • The firm runs without you
  • Your income is no longer tied to your personal hours
  • Your time is yours to spend however you choose
  • This is where true freedom and true wealth become possible
The gap between where you are and where you want to be is not about your expertise — it is about the systems you have not built yet, and that is exactly what this plan addresses.
Overall Growth Stage Assessment
PS Law Group
PS Law Group is operating at Stage 3 — a solo practitioner in structure despite having a small team, because every growth-critical function still flows through Paula. She has built real expertise, genuine market differentiation, and growing revenue in one of the most complex practice areas in the country — entirely on word-of-mouth. The single most urgent gap is the absence of both a predictable lead generation system and operational infrastructure: while competitors like Greenspoon Marder and Mr. Cannabis Law invest in digital visibility and content authority, PS Law Group remains invisible online to the B2B clients actively searching for exactly what Paula does best. Until she has the systems, the delegation structure, and the financial clarity to step back from the day-to-day, the 10-attorney firm she envisions — and the AI company she is building alongside it — will remain out of reach.
