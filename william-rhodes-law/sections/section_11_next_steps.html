Consolidate Domains and Fix NAP Consistency Across Directories
Lead Gen Quick Win
Why It Matters
The firm operates under two brand names and two domains (williamsrhodeslaw.com and ericrhodeslaw.com) with no confirmed redirect. Google sees a split identity and suppresses local rankings for both. Every major directory still references the old name and old website.
The Opportunity
A proper 301 redirect from ericrhodeslaw.com to williamsrhodeslaw.com, combined with updated NAP citations across all directories, consolidates search authority to the new brand and can lift organic visibility within 30–60 days.
