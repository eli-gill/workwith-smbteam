Why It Matters

Implement Automated Inquiry Acknowledgment and Follow-Up Sequence
Intake Quick Win
Why It Matters
Prospects who submit a contact form or call after hours and receive no immediate acknowledgment routinely call a competitor before morning. Rose cannot be available for every first contact — but an automated system can respond instantly, confirm the inquiry was received, and set expectations for next steps.
The Opportunity

Deploy AI Lead Screening with Live Agent Backup for After-Hours Coverage
Intake Long-Term Play
Why It Matters
The firm's intake currently flows entirely through Rose — which means every lead that arrives while she is unavailable either waits or leaves. At 8 cases per month, this is manageable. At 16 cases per month, it is not. The intake system must scale before the lead volume does.
The Opportunity
