Lead Generation

The Grande Law Firm has grown to over $1 million in revenue on the strength of referrals and organic Google traffic — but lead volume has been declining for five months and no paid advertising has ever been activated. The firm's primary competitors are building review equity, owning multiple domain properties targeting the same Torrance immigration keywords, and establishing Spanish-language brands that reach directly into the working-class ICP communities where this firm's growth opportunity lives.

Website Audit

The Problem
  • Homepage title and H1 are not aligned to "Immigration Attorney in Torrance, CA" — the firm's primary service and primary city are not signaled at the top of the page where Google and visitors look first
  • PageSpeed scores could not be confirmed during research — the site should be tested at pagespeed.web.dev, as even a newly built WordPress site can load slowly due to unoptimized images or plugins, and mobile performance directly impacts both SEO rankings and conversion rate for Spanish-speaking ICP visitors using phones as their primary device
  • No prominent click-to-call phone number in the top-right header — visitors who want to call immediately must search for the number
  • No NAP (name, address, phone) with embedded map in the footer — a foundational local SEO signal that Google uses to verify location relevance for both the Torrance and San Bruno offices
Blind Spot: The Spanish language toggle exists on the site but is not prominently positioned — Spanish-speaking visitors who need help most may miss it entirely and leave before they ever contact the firm. Making it highly visible is a simple fix with a direct impact on the ICP the firm is trying to reach.
The Positive
  • Newly redesigned site with a modern, professional presentation — not a trust-eroding design problem
  • Multiple geo-targeted landing pages already built and indexed — /torrance-immigration-lawyer/, /los-angeles-immigration-lawyer/, /immigration-attorneys-carson/, /bankruptcy-attorneys-torrance/ — providing a local SEO foundation to build on
The Gap
  • Without a click-to-call phone in the header, mobile visitors — particularly working-class Spanish speakers using phones as primary devices — leave without contacting the firm at the moment of highest intent
  • Missing footer NAP creates an SEO signal gap that contributes to lower local pack relevance, especially for searches with geographic intent like "immigration attorney near Torrance"
  • Title and H1 misalignment means Google is not ranking this site as the most relevant result for the primary keyword the firm needs to own — costing organic visibility that compounds monthly
Every structural conversion gap on this website is a lead that never arrives — and a firm that cannot generate reliable leads cannot run without its owner.
