Here Is Where Paramount Law Group Stands Today

Donna Gee has built Paramount Law Group into a ten-person Calgary practice over the past decade, handling estates and probate, guardianship and trusteeship, corporate and commercial matters, and select family law files. The firm bills over $1.2 million per year and has earned strong client loyalty through referrals and Donna's community presence — but it runs almost entirely on her personal capacity, with no paid marketing, only 13 Google reviews, and a website that was deliberately left underdeveloped. The most critical finding: Donna has the revenue, the reputation, and the ambition to build a firm she can sell — but the systems, team structure, and marketing presence needed to make that happen do not yet exist.

Stage 6 is the stage where Donna can spend two months a year in Mexico, keep a Tuesday-through-Thursday schedule, and own a firm that builds value without her — which is exactly what she has described, and exactly where we are taking her.
